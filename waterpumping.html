We have our complete autonomous water pumping and distribution solution based on our wisenet and installed at our customers' site. This solution is for large facilities spawning over multiple acres of area. We have our own end devices for this solution comprising
  • Level Sensors
  • Electric Butterfly Valves
  • Solenoid Valves
  • Single Phase and Three Phase Motor Interfaces.
Our solution facilitates total autonomous operation to fully manual remote control capability. This solution is directly applicable to any liquids and gases requirement.

Customer quote "We could reduce man power requirement while providing water to different hostels 24 hours every day from shared motors and tanks. We could save 25% of water by avoiding manual operation and related inefficiency."
